starling.predictor.linear_pilot.LinearPilot¶

class starling.predictor.linear_pilot.LinearPilot(environment: starling.core.environment.Environment, step_time: float, fix_proximity_threshold: float)[source]¶

Bases: starling.predictor.predictor.Predictor

Evolves the Aircraft with constant speed and vertical speed along it’s heading. If aircraft is in Route following mode, the heading is updated accordingly.

Aircraft can be instructed to go directly to any Fix on its Route by giving a set_route command. Aircraft also responds to change_heading_* actions. If Aircraft is “on heading”, it continues on that heading until an on_route or set_route action is issued, returning the Aircraft to Route following behaviour.
