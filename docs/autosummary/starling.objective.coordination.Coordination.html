Evaluate the penalty for deviating from the coordinated exit point.
evaluate(environment: starling.core.environment.Environment, trajectories: Dict[str, starling.core.trajectory.Trajectory]) → starling.core.reward.Reward[source]

Evaluate the penalty for deviating from the coordinated exit point. Any vertical difference is exponentially penalised. Any lateral difference above 5nm is exponentially penalised. This objective takes non-zero values only when at least one aircraft has exited the “outer boundary” of the sector (see Starling issue #148 for definition) and its passed trajectory intersects with the outer boundary. Any aircraft whose trajectories do not intersect with the outer boundary are given zero penalty (with a warning issued).
