starling.utility.bada.energy_share_factor

starling.utility.bada.energy_share_factor(flight_condition: str, mach: float, temperature: float, delta_t: float) → float[source]

Given a mach number calculate energy share factor. See equations 3.2-8 to 3.2-11 in EEC Technical/Scientific Report No. 2010-003.

Parameters

  • flight_condition (The) – The flight condition of the aircraft

  • mach (float) – The aircrafts speed as a mach number (dimensionless)

  • temperature (float) – The atmospheric temperature at height of the aircraft (in K)

  • delta_t (float) – The BADA atmosphere temperature differential at a pressure altitude of zero (in K)

Returns

The energy share factor in BADA ROCD equation (dimensionless)

Return type

float

Raises

ValueError – If the aircraft state is not recognised
