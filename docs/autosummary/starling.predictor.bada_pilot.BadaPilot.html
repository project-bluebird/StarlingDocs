starling.predictor.bada_pilot.BadaPilot

class starling.predictor.bada_pilot.BadaPilot(environment: starling.core.environment.Environment, step_time: float, fix_proximity_threshold: float)[source]

Bases: starling.predictor.predictor.Predictor

Evolves the Aircraft with constant speed along it’s heading. If aircraft is in Route following mode, the heading is updated accordingly.

Aircraft can be instructed to go directly to any Fix on its Route by giving a set_route command. Aircraft also responds to change_heading_* actions. If Aircraft is “on heading”, it continues on that heading until an on_route or set_route action is issued, returning the Aircraft to Route following behaviour.

The rate of climb or descent is determined using a BADA model implementation

update_flight_level(callsign, step)[source]

Update aircraft’s flight level based on BADA model. Here we assume an ISA (International Standard Atmospehere) at position of the aircraft. In future might consider different BADA atmospheres, i.e. delta_T and delta_P values other than zero.

Note: We are rounding the change in flight level to nearest whole number. This rounding procedure needs careful future consideration in terms what ATCs would want from a built-in safety perspective.
